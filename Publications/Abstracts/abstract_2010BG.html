Approximation of the Eigenvalue Problem for Time Harmonic Maxwell System by Continuous Lagrange Finite Elements.
Bonito, A. and Guermond, J.-L.

We propose and analyze an approximation technique for the Maxwell eigenvalue problem using H1-conforming finite elements. The key idea consists of controlling the divergence of the electric field in a fractional Sobolev space H-α with α ∈ (½,1). The method is shown to be convergent and spectrally correct.
