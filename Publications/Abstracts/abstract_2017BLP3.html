On Sinc Quadrature Approximations of Fractional Powers of Regularly Accretive Operators.
Bonito, A. and Lei, Wenyu and Pasciak, J.E.

We consider the finite element approximation of fractional powers of regularly accretive operators via the Dunford-Taylor integral approach. We use a sinc quadrature scheme to approximate the Balakrishnan representation of the negative powers of the operator as well as its finite element approximation. We improve the exponentially convergent error estimates from [A. Bonito, J. E. Pasciak, IMA J. Numer. Anal. (2016) 00, 1-29] by reducing the regularity required on the data.
