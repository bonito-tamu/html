Regularity of the Maxwell Equations in Heterogeneous Medio and Lipschitz Domains
Bonito, A. and Guermond, J.-L. and Luddens, F.

This note establishes regularity estimates for the solution of the Maxwell equations in Lipschitz domains with non-smooth coecients and min- imal regularity assumptions. The argumentation relies on elliptic regularity estimates for the Poisson problem with non-smooth coecients which are de- rived as well.
