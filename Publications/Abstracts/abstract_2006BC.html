A Remark on a Theorem of Dore Concerning Lp Maximal Regularity.
Bonito, A. and Clement, Ph.

The aim of this note is to show that for the necessary conditions in Theorems 2.1 and 2.2 in Dore (1991), the operator A does not have to be densely defined. Evenmore, when the space X is reflexive this assumption becomes a conclusion. An application to the regularity of the Stokes problem is given.
