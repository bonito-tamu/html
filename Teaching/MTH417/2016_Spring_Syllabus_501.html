Course description and prerequisites

This is a one-semester course on numerical numerical analysis which gives an introduction to various topics in numerical methods. In particular, the class covers
  1. iterative methods in one dimension,
  2. interpolation and polynomial approximation,
  3. numerical differentiation and integration,
  4. direct methods for linear systems,
  5. iterative methods for linear systems,
  6. numerical approximation of ordinary differential equations,
  7. extra topics (if time permits).

Note that it is impossible to cover all the material provided in the textbook and some material will be omitted.

Minimal Outcome

This course is to provide students with basic, yet critical, tools in numerical analysis and scientific computation. The students should be able to master their principles, understand their mathematical properties and implement them in matlab.
